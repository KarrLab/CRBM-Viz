1. Development team arrow_upward

BioSimulations was developed by Bilal Shaikh and Jonathan Karr at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai and Akhil Marupilla, Mike Wilson , , and Ion Moraru at the Center for Cell Analysis and Modeling at the University of Connecticut Health Center.

2. Partners arrow_upward

BioSimulations will support a wide range of community standards and resources including several modeling languages (CellML , NeuroML , pharmML , SBML ), experiment description languages (SED-ML ), other standards (OMEX ), model repositories ( BioModels , Open Source Brain , Physiome ), simulators (COBRApy, COPASI , libRoadRunner, NFSim, OpenCOR, VCell ), and data visualization grammars ( Vega , Vega-lite ).

3. Technology arrow_upward

BioSimulations is implemented using the following technologies:

  • Web frameworks: Angular , Flask , GraphQL
  • HTML components: Material Design ag-Grid
  • Theme: Material Theme Generator
  • Icons: Font Awesome , Material Design
  • Authentication: Auth0
  • Containers: Docker Kubernetes
  • Deployment: Heroku
  • Source code version control: GitHub
  • Continuous integration: GitHub
